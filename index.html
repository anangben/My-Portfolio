Farm Assist

Built With: Node.js, Express, MongoDB

What It Is: A backend system for an agri-commerce marketplace platform that connects farmers to buyers. It includes secure user authentication, role-based access control, product management for farmers, order placement for buyers, and administrative tools for managing users, vendors, and platform operations.

-> Account authentication with sign-up, login, and password reset for users (individuals, businesses, distributors) and vendors (farmers).
-> Vendors can list, update, and manage their farm products.
-> Allow users (individuals, businesses, distributors) to browse products and place orders.
-> Superadmin role with full access to manage users and vendors, oversee payments, and handle overall platform administration.
